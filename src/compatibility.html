What does vital shirt app
compatibility mean?
The Vital Shirt is designed for use with most Apps that run on devices able to accept an ANT + or Bluetooth 4.0 (or newer) connection. This includes but is not limited to:
Strava
Trainingpeaks
Zwift
EliteHRV
Polar Beat
Polar Flow
Nike Run Club
MapMyFitness
RunKeeper
Apple Health
Google Fit
Garmin Connect

Your favorite app is not on the list? Please try to connect the Vitalshirt via Bluetooth - if it works, let us know and we will add it to the list as soon as possible!
