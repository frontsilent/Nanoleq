The Vital Shirt centres around the one who wears it. With a diverse range of customization and compatibility options with devices and apps, you will keep this powerful tool close to your heart in no time.

Voltage versus time.
The electrical activity of your heart.

Using state of the art electrodes, the Vital Shirt is able to detect small electrical changes that are a result of cardiac muscle depolarization followed by repolarization during each heartbeat. The Vital Shirt processes these information signals to produce a gold standard Electrocardiogram (ECG), providing you with valuable insights into your heart and health.
