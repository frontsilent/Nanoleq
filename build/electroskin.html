Why ELECTROSKIN?

Nanoleq is the creator of ElectroSkin, a hybrid polymer-textile electrode solution. It’s the only textile electrode on the market with consistently low skin impedance and strong signal quality from the first seconds of dry skin contact until >100 washing and wearing cycles. ElectroSkin is ultrathin, elastic and still performs outstandingly after many times washing and wearing. It can be integrated onto garments in a one-step lamination process. Connecting ElectroSkin is fast and reliable thanks to a smart connection interface. Textile manufacturers can now easily form electrical connections, without electronics expertise and without the need for crimping or soldering.

ElectroSkin GECKO

Nanoleq’s flagship product is the ElectroSkin GECKO, which comes with a skin-adhesive surface. This GECKO effect causes the electrode to be naturally attracted to the skin. This prevents unwanted electrode lift-off and allows for excellent signal quality without making the garment overtight.

The skin contact surface and elastic modulus of ElectroSkin STIM is optimal for comfortable electrostimulation for long lasting e-textiles.

ElectroSkin Starter Kit

The ElectroSkin Starter Kit contains all you need to equip your e-textile with the best performing skin electrodes for textiles. ElectroSkin electrodes are light, thin and their skin impedance is even better than standard medical gel electrodes, without wetting and even after repeated washing. The kit comes with textile connector components and tools to laminate ElectroSkin electrodes onto textiles and connect them to your electronic module.

Necessary textile workshop tools for handling lamination and buttons: Hot press or lamination machine (for starters, an iron works as well), textile button press & hole puncher.

Ingredients:

  • 12 ElectroSkin electrodes
  • Insulation patches for connections to buttons
  • Conductive snap buttons & tools
  • Supporting tools for press buttons
  • Laminated reference sample
  • SOP documentation
