Get in Touch
Because we are engineers at heart, we like to design, produce and test our products ourselves. We make sure to offer exceptionally high product quality, as we believe this is key to keep our customers happy.

Production capabilities

Over the last years, we have established our own automated production in the heart of Zurich. Our fabrication equipment spans from compounding, extrusion and screen-printing to textile lamination and testing equipment. With our prototyping and production capabilities, we are flexible and ready to support bold and challenging requests from our textile customers.

Engineering services

With several years of experience in the field, the Nanoleq team is able to engineer and deliver full end-to-end solution for biosignal monitoring applied to textiles. To date, our technologies can be found in a wide range of consumer, workwear and medical applications. Through our vast network of textile and electronics manufacturers, software and cloud providers, we can provide you with a ready-to-market product.

Laboratory testing

Over the years, Nanoleq has not only built truly reliable, washable and comfortable technology for smart textiles but also established numerous testing protocols for products to satisfy industry requirements.

To transform your functional prototype into a reliable product, we can provide insight into your product on the following aspects:

Basic Textile Choice
  • Selection of materials
  • Strength
  • Abrasion
  • Pilling resistance
  • Colorfastness
  • Stability to washing and dry cleaning
Electronics Robustness
  • Stretching
  • Abrasion
  • Shear
  • Stress
Chemical Resistance
  • Washing Cycles
  • Swear Exposure
  • Prolonged Wearing
Biocompatibility Testing
  • Cytotoxicity ISO 10993-5
  • Skin irritation ISO 10993-10
We indicate how your product relates to comparable systems and we advise you how to further improve the reliability.
